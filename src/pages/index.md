Our lab is located in the [Department of Biology](http://bio.as.uky.edu/) at the
[University of Kentucky](http://www.uky.edu/).

We are interested in using mathematical and computational tools to study
conceptual questions in evolutionary biology and ecology. A main focus of work in
the group is the evolution of complex phenotypes including social behavior,
phenotypic plasticity, and traits on rough fitness landscapes.
